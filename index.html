Who We Are

The purpose of this site is to move money from white and non-Black POC comrades to Black and Indigenous comrades in the Cornell and Ithaca community in the form of reparations. Adapted from Students Organizing Mutual Aid and Defense (SOMAD) -- thank you!

Receive Reparations

BIPOC - Start Getting Your Reparations

Fill out the form below and get your reparations! We will anonymize your name and add you to the sheet below. No need to remove your name once you receive money, we want you to continue to receive what is yours over time.

Give Reparations

White and NBPOC - Give Back Your Stolen Wealth

Select folks on the list to give generously to, using what cash transfer mediums a given reparations recipient has listed. In order to try to maximize the people on this list who get reparations, we encourage white and NBPOC people to select recipients randomly, and to give generously to multiple people in the Cornell and Ithaca community. We want to point out that, Resource Generation guides people in the top 10% to start the journey of redistributing stolen wealth by redistributing 10% of their wealth monthly, with the goal of redistributing all or almost all inherited wealth over time. If you're concerned about the "unrandomness" of picking recipients, feel free to use this random number generator.
